porcelain crown

Porcelain crown is a prosthetic technique in dentistry that helps to reconstruct the aesthetics and function of teeth. Relate to When it comes to the solution of porcelain dental crowns, there are many questions surrounding such as: does porcelain crown hurt, how long will it last? And how much does a porcelain crown cost? To find out the detailed answer, please learn more through article below.

1. What is the method of porcelain crown ?

Porcelain tooth or porcelain crown is a type of denture with the shape, size and color that is made almost like real teeth. but empty inside. The doctor will use a porcelain crown to cover the real tooth to help restore its shape, function and aesthetics for teeth.

When covering porcelain teeth, the doctor will grind most of the real tooth tissue of the tooth to create a pulp, so this is an invasive method of tooth tissue quite a lot.

2. Why is porcelain teeth popular?

Porcelain veneers are popular thanks to 4 outstanding advantages:

2.1. Ensure high aesthetics

Due to the shape and color of real teeth, this is a solution to restore large broken teeth, teeth that have undergone root canal treatment, and damaged teeth. weakened by tooth decay… as well as teeth with effective aesthetic defects. Currently, besides porcelain crowns, there are also There is a solution of porcelain veneers to help limit the invasion of teeth better, so please consult your doctor carefully for a comprehensive solution to your smile.

2.2. High durability

If using the right good porcelain material, having a clear origin, certified quality and treated by a Doctor With the right technique, the time to use porcelain can be up to 8-10 years. Thanks to the rigidity, the function chewing of the teeth is still guaranteed throughout the period of use.

2.3. Quick porcelain coating time

Usually, porcelain teeth treatment lasts from 3-7 days with 3-5 appointments. Depending on the dental condition, the number The number of teeth that need to be covered with porcelain, the qualifications of the Doctor and the facilities of the clinic, sometimes time is available can be faster or slower.

Nowadays, thanks to the application of CAD/CAM digital technology, we can restore large broken teeth by covering porcelain teeth in just a few hours, saving customers time and effort.

3. When should get porcelain veneers?

  • When the tooth is decayed, inflamed, or broken.
  • Teeth has some cosmetic problems such as: heavy tetra/fluorine staining, sparse/cleft teeth, slight deviation.
  • Lost teeth and customers want to restore with porcelain bridge solution.

For the best results, you should go to reputable dental centers to examine and consult with your doctor about your condition. Is the condition of my teeth suitable for porcelain crown solution.

4. Making porcelain teeth painful?

Does porcelain crowns hurt largely depends on the skill of the doctor and the process of making porcelain teeth at that clinic. Specifically:

  • The doctor needs to examine carefully to detect and completely treat oral diseases such as pulpitis, caries. teeth before grinding porcelain teeth.
  • Precision grinding technique.
  • The technique of fitting porcelain crowns tightly to the roots of the teeth, without causing tension or clumps.
  • Check the bite thoroughly after you're done.
