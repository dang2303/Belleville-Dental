planting dental implants, process

Implant implant (implantation implant) is the most advanced method of replacing missing teeth today with abutments Implants are implanted into the jawbone to replace the lost tooth roots. Thereby ensuring the function of chewing often as well as help patients keep their smile, face and health aesthetic.

1. What is Implant ?

Engine Implant, also known as Implant or Implant, is an advanced denture implant method The most effective in modern medicine, helping to restore effectively most cases of tooth loss.

When making Implant teeth, the doctor will perform surgery to place the Implant posts (artificial roots) into the bone jaw to replace the old lost tooth roots. After the Implant post is biocompatible with the body, a porcelain crown Similar to real teeth, it will be attached on top, helping you to eat and chew comfortably like real teeth, and at the same time get it back Harmonious aesthetics, beautiful smile and more confidence.

2. Implant implant procedure medical standard

Normally, the process of implanting teeth will go through 4 steps as follows:

Step 1: Examine, take pictures, take movies to survey the condition of teeth and jawbone. From there, sketch out the plan treatment.

Before placing the Implant, you will have a general examination of your oral condition, take a CBCT film to know bone density, jaw bone thickness. Based on the results obtained, the doctor will advise and make a plan plan Appropriate treatment, cost as well as time to make Implant teeth.

Step 2: Blood test and implant surgery.

Before implanting the Implant, the doctor will assign the necessary blood tests to ensure the condition status best health. Then proceed to implant surgery. The patient will be anesthetized/pre-anesthetic throughout this process. In the case of loss of all teeth and incisors, temporary teeth will be made while wait Implant cylinders integrate biologically with the body, to ensure aesthetics and help patients eat and chew easily than.

Step 3: Re-examination, check the condition of teeth after surgery.

Follow-up examination as prescribed for the doctor to check the healing.

Step 4: Restoration of porcelain teeth on Implant.

After about 6-14 weeks, the Implant post was integrated, the doctor proceeded to install porcelain teeth on the abutment to complete. socks dental implant procedure. This stage usually takes place in 3-5 appointments within 5 minutes – 7 days depending on the condition of each tooth.
