Children's Dentistry

Children's dentistry includes care, monitoring and treatment of problems related to oral health for children in the age of baby teeth and the replacement of permanent teeth, which can start with children from 3 to 3 years old. 15 years old. Choosing a high-quality children's dental clinic will give your child a comfortable oral care experience, a healthy, beautiful smile and complete confidence.

1. Dental for children baby teeth stage

All the concerns at this stage are to keep the baby's teeth clean to avoid baby tooth decay. Keeping baby teeth healthy not only helps them have a good experience for talking and chewing activities but also an important premise for the development of permanent teeth at the time of tooth replacement. At 2 years old, children can start brushing their teeth with a pea-sized amount of toothpaste and a soft-bristled toothbrush. By the age of 3, the amount of toothpaste will increase to the size of a pea twice a day. At the age of 3, dentists encourage parents to give their children their first dental visit. This trip is for the dentist to check to help the child keep their teeth clean and also a way for the child to get used to the dental environment and create a habit of going to the dentist every 6 months for a dental checkup.

2. Children's dentistry the period of tooth replacement (permanent teething)

At the age of 6, children prepare to replace their first tooth. Baby teeth are in turn replaced by permanent teeth. This stage is really important, your child's smile and oral health for the rest of his life will depend on his or her teeth replacement journey being properly cared for.

Your child's order of tooth replacement begins with the eruption of the molar (6th tooth) and ends with the 7th tooth at the age of 12.

Following up the process of tooth replacement and regular dental checkups for babies helps parents early detect abnormalities that are easy to encounter during this period such as lack of tooth germ, extra teeth in the middle, teeth that grow underground (Mesiodens). interferes with the eruption of teeth and affects the position of the remaining teeth.

At the same time, 6-12 years old is also the time when the jawbone develops strongly. The deviations and aberrations of the jaw bone such as protrusion, underbite, narrow jaw with crowded teeth will be intervened at the right time, taken care of and completely overcome these deviations.

3. Các vấn đề nha khoa trẻ em thường gặp ở độ tuổi thay răng

Dưới đây là những vấn đề về răng thường gặp ở trẻ nhỏ mà Ba Mẹ cần lưu ý:

  • Sâu răng: Khi trẻ xuất hiện sâu răng, vết sâu nên được điều trị sớm kết hợp cùng vệ sinh răng kỹ lưỡng để giữ gìn và bảo tồn răng cho trẻ.
  • Trình tự thay răng: Quá trình thay răng có rất nhiều vấn đề có thể xảy ra, các mầm răng vĩnh viễn có đủ hay không, có ở vị trí đúng hay không. Trẻ cần được khảo sát và theo dõi thay răng giúp các răng được mọc đúng vị trí.
  • Hô, móm, răng chen chúc lệch lạc: Hô xương, móm xương, các răng mọc lên không ngay ngắn gây khó khăn trong ăn nhai, phát âm và ảnh hưởng đến tự tin của trẻ.
  • ...

Những vấn đề này cần điều trị “chữa lành” càng sớm càng tốt và nên bắt đầu trong giai đoạn thay răng khi trẻ 6 – 12 tuổi. 5. Treatments pediatric dentistry

According to statistics, more than 90% of Vietnamese children are having dental problems. In which, more than 85% of children from 6 to 8 years old have tooth decay, 80-90% of children have gingivitis, 75% have problems with misaligned teeth and malocclusion. Children's dental treatments include:

5.1. Deep tooth filling

Currently, fillings for children often use GIC material, which releases Fluor to help remineralize decayed teeth. Not only that, the GIC material has good adhesion on the baby tooth tissue, making the filling operation faster because it is difficult for children to cooperate unlike adults. In addition, Sealant can be used to prevent caries in the chewing surfaces of the posterior teeth.

5.2. Minor surgery for tooth extraction

Some minor dental surgeries should be performed at a children's dental clinic, such as extracting baby teeth during the replacement period, there are permanent teeth that grow while baby teeth have not fallen, need to extract baby teeth to make room for permanent teeth. favorable growth.

In case it is found that a child has extra teeth that grow underground inside the bone, also known as Mesioden teeth, which interferes with the eruption of the permanent teeth, causing the baby teeth to fall out for a long time but the permanent teeth have not yet erupted, the child needs to practice perform minor surgery to remove the underground tooth to make way for the permanent tooth to erupt normally.

Some children at the age of 10-12 years old have sprouted wisdom teeth (tooth number 8), while tooth number 7 (molar tooth) has not yet erupted, causing tooth 7 to be stuck. They will need evaluation by an orthodontist. have the experience and expertise to handle.

5.3. Correction

The deviations related to the jawbone, bite such as: overbite, underbite, open bite, deep bite, misaligned teeth, the doctor will perform orthodontics, braces, bring back the correct bite.
