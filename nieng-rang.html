Braces

Braces are one of the optimal orthodontic methods today, effectively overcoming the condition of teeth sparse, ho, crooked, deviated, helping you to have a healthy and confident smile. However, to end the editing journey, If you want to succeed, you should learn very well about the types of braces, costs, time and important notes important.

1. What is the method of braces ?

Braces (also known as orthodontics) are methods of using specialized dental appliances to translate Move the misalignment of teeth and jawbone to the correct position. Specifically, adjusting the tooth structure, teeth crooked, crowded, protruding teeth… or deviations related to bite and jawbone such as deep bite, reverse bite, cross bite, open bite, overbite, underbite... bring a new, straight and firm smile healthy, aesthetic relationship between lips - nose - chin is harmonious and balanced.

For young children, the GOLDEN age for braces is 6-12 years old when the child is in the age of tooth replacement. This is the time The point of the jawbone is in the process of developing with the replacement of teeth, so the adjustment of the bite is wrong Misalignment of the correct position or alignment of teeth will be easy, effective and provide comprehensive results both in terms of aesthetic smile, face and chewing function of children.

For adults, braces should be done as soon as possible so that the teeth can be corrected false positives, bring confidence, prevent dental complications and protect healthy teeth. Braces are the way to maintain oral health, so from 6 years old and up, any age can be brace. For people 45-50 years old, have the desire to get braces but still hesitate to get in trouble Then braces with Invisalign transparent aligners is a very suitable method.

2. Benefits of orthodontic braces

2.1. Adjust the bite standard

Correction of deep bite, overbite, crossbite and open bite. Help put joints bite to the correct physiological standard, ensuring the balance between the upper and lower jaw.

2.2. Improve chewing function

The bite is shifted to physiological standards and the teeth are even, making jaw movements more rhythmic, continuing The chewing force of the upper and lower jaws is adequate, improving chewing ability significantly.

2.3. Prevention of dental disease

Orthodontics straightens teeth, correct bite, makes it easier to clean teeth, thereby preventing Effective prevention of oral diseases such as tooth decay, periodontitis, gingivitis ...

2.4. Shine with a beautiful smile

An even teeth, no longer crowded, with physiological standard bite will create a natural smile line. perfect nature, helping you to be confident when communicating with people around.

2.5. Change face appearance

Not only will the misalignment of the bite and uneven teeth be corrected, the jawbone structure will also be improved adjusted, creating the feeling that your face becomes slimmer and more harmonious than before.

3. The braces process go through what stages?

Step 1: Examination & Filming

First, the doctor will conduct a general examination to assess your needs as well as learn the medical history dental disease (if any). Next, you'll be photographed inside your mouth, your face as well as your film X-rays, cephalometric films… so that the orthodontist has the necessary data for the diagnosis.

Step 2: Consultation, treatment plan

Based on film data, photos and other supporting software, the doctor will determine the wrong condition deviation and give a treatment plan as well as predict the duration of treatment, then advise you carefully next program.

When you agree to treatment, you need to take an impression of your teeth. With the method of orthodontic braces, the impression is still according to the traditional process. With Invisalign, you will experience the current 3D tooth scanning technology great.

Step 3: Manufacture of tools

After you have agreed to the treatment plan, the braces support devices will be carried out. manufacturing. Depending on the method of braces, the process will be different. Braces can be done right away with placement of the appliance in the mouth. With Invisalign clear braces, the process has more steps. From a dental scan, it took about 3 weeks for the doctor to make a treatment plan and simulate it on Clincheck. Afterward About 3-4 weeks, your set of aligners will be finished and available at the clinic for you to receive the first trays.

Step 4: Put on the appliance

Your doctor will put on or instruct you to wear the appliance yourself.

Step 5: Re-examination, adjustment, control

Re-examination as indicated for the doctor to adjust and control the movement of teeth and bite.

Step 6: Wear a retainer to protect your new smile

After achieving the perfect orthodontic effect: uniform teeth, physiological standard bite and balanced face For example, you will wear retainers for 1-2 years. The retainer has the effect of stabilizing the teeth in the new position and prevent the periodontal ligaments from moving, keeping the orthodontic result fixed.
