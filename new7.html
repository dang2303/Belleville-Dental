What is dental porcelain implant, when should it be done?

Porcelain dental implant is the solution prescribed by doctors when The patient has lost all teeth in the jaw or lost too many teeth in the jaw. In this case, the ability to chew is often reduced, leading to Food must not be crushed before entering the stomach. In the long term, You are at risk of developing a number of diseases related to the digestive tract. Besides, if the loss of teeth is not corrected in time will lead to jawbone loss as well as make the face rapidly aging.

According to dentists, planting original porcelain is only done When the patient's health condition is guaranteed, oral health good and got some problems like:

  • Loss of many teeth, even loss of full teeth.
  • People with severe periodontitis, loose teeth need required to have all teeth extracted.

Basically, planting original porcelain will help overcome some problems Above, bring the desired teeth to help the owner feel more confident in communicate.

List of types of porcelain teeth commonly used today

What porcelain material is used to plant primitive porcelain? Let's join together Refer to some types of porcelain teeth commonly used today:

Metal porcelain teeth

Implant metal porcelain teeth is the solution chosen by many customers selected, are divided into two main types are ordinary metal teeth and titanium metal teeth.

Normal metal teeth

Metal porcelain teeth usually have an internal structure consisting of chromium - Cobalt or Chromium-Nickel. Meanwhile the outside surface is coated a layer of porcelain should have the same color as natural teeth. Type of porcelain teeth This has quite good bite resistance, low cost, so it is suitable for wide range of customers. Durability of popular metal porcelain teeth from 3 to 5 years.

  • Advantages: Lowest cost of all porcelain teeth while the durability is equivalent to real teeth, ensuring chewing sure.
  • Cons: Teeth is usually milky white, not clear, yes The ability to change color and gradually blacken the neck of the teeth close to the posterior gum line a period of use due to the metal frame being oxidized. Copper In the past, teeth are less natural, easily detected as false teeth when there is light light shines through.
Titanium porcelain teeth

Titanium porcelain teeth also have a similar structure to metal porcelain teeth, but However, the inside is covered with a layer of titanium. This is the material used Commonly used in medicine because it is hypoallergenic and can be combined Good with the skeletal organization of the body. If you choose to plant porcelain teeth In this original jaw, the crown is usually slightly opaque and shiny not as natural as all-porcelain teeth. However, the strength of the teeth is also quite high with about 10 years or more.

  • Advantages: After installation, titanium porcelain teeth are quite similar to Real teeth, ensuring aesthetic value. Besides, Titanium has good biocompatibility, does not cause irritation to the body body. Teeth also have high strength, ensuring the chewing process user's.
  • Disadvantage: The frame of the tooth still uses composite material Metallic metal so when irradiated it still has a black luster. Similar Like normal metal porcelain teeth, after a period of use the teeth are still blackened gingival margin due to the action of oxidation reaction.
Porcelain dentures with all-ceramic material

All-porcelain teeth are teeth made entirely of porcelain, not containing any other metal components. This is also considered a substance Today's leading material, often used when planting teeth door or plant original porcelain.

  • Advantages: All-porcelain teeth have beautiful natural colors, clear, smooth and even like real teeth. Even with the light, the teeth are still colored bright white. After a period of use, teeth usually do not appear black gingival margin or cleft. The durability of the teeth is also quite high, can last up to decades.
  • Cons: The cost of making all-ceramic teeth is often quite high, requiring Ask the tooth design and manufacturing process performed by the company Modern technology, the most advanced machinery system. You also need to choose Choose a reputable dental address, the doctor needs to be skilled high because the stages of growing porcelain are very complicated and meticulous.

Does full dentures hurt? Execution process details

To plant whole teeth, you need to apply the implant method Implant instead of porcelain bridge. Because, to make a porcelain bridge requires The 2 adjacent teeth must be healthy to be able to make a post. So, If you lose all your teeth, you will need to use an implant directly into the jawbone.

Is dental implantation painful or not is always a concern of everyone customer before implementation. With this technique, the patient will Local anesthetic in the area to be implanted. Therefore, the patient will I only feel a little numb feeling when inserting the porcelain dental abutment. The rest, the implementation steps will take place quite gently with time It takes about 10-15 minutes for 1 implant post.

After you leave, you will be given more pain and cold medicine The pain will gradually decrease and disappear in about 2-3 days there. The process of implanting full-molar porcelain teeth will be done with 5 Following are the basic steps:

  • Step 1:First, the doctor will examine and check the condition overall health status as well as requesting you to provide information Information about your medical history, especially if you have a medical condition such as urinary blood sugar, blood pressure, heart rate or not. When you realize you have enough In the event of a porcelain dental implant, the doctor will perform an X-ray to check the dental condition.
  • Step 2: Through basic examination and scan results X-ray, the doctor will know the dental diseases that need treatment treatment and defects that need to be rectified. Then the doctor will give decision making, treatment planning and counseling Choose the right type of porcelain teeth.
  • Step 3: You will have a clean oral cavity to make sure bacteria do not enter the process. After There, the doctor will perform surgery to place the implant posts in in the jawbone with a time of about 20-30 minutes/teeth.
  • Step 4: After about 1-3 months depending on the compatibility If you like the implant tooth with the jaw bone, the doctor will take an impression of the tooth implant and conduct the process of making porcelain teeth. To perform, Dentists use specialized synthetic materials to take impressions of teeth. After that, the tooth impression tray will be sent to the lab for processing Porcelain dental work is suitable for each patient.
  • Step 5: Porcelain teeth will be made in 1 up to 2 days. After the above time, you return to the clinic to proceed to attach fixed porcelain teeth to the implant teeth and complete the process dental implant.

After completing the process of planting porcelain teeth, the doctor will give you a time follow-up time to monitor the patient's condition.

Instructions for effective care after porcelain teeth implant

After implanting full-molar porcelain teeth, the teeth will ensure the ability to eat chewing as well as good bearing capacity like real teeth. Besides that, teeth also have a natural color, the right size to bring highest aesthetics. However, you should be especially careful when take care and clean teeth after cosmetic surgery to prolong life, help Porcelain teeth are durable, avoid being stained by specific foods:

  • Limit eating foods that are too hard, need to use strong bite force to Avoid causing breakage, chipping, broken teeth.
  • Within 24 hours after implanting full-fledged porcelain teeth, you should avoid eating foods that are too cold, too hot, or too acidic to prevent toothache frost, affecting the porcelain enamel layer.
  • Limit the use of foods with dark colors that easily stick to teeth such as coffee, tea, carbonated soft drinks... After using, you need to beat teeth, gargle early.
  • Do not smoke, drink alcohol because this is the cause damage tooth enamel, causing porcelain teeth to become yellow.
  • Clean teeth regularly every day with a table Specialized soft brush. When brushing your teeth, move the brush gently gently vertically, then focus on rotating the brush inward chewing surface.
  • After brushing your teeth, you need to use dilute salt water to rinse your mouth Add dental floss to remove plaque on teeth and interdental teeth.
  • Visit your dentist every 6 months for a check-up and eliminate the risk of causing dental disease at the earliest.
