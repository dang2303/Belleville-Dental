What's a crooked look? Who is it suitable for?

Normally, an adult will have 32 divided teeth into types: incisors, canines, premolars and molars. The crooked tooth is actually another name for the canine tooth (tooth number 3). misaligned.

Instead of growing straight, as usual, crooked teeth will grow outward at an angle of about 5 to 10 degrees. So understand simple Simply put, planting a canine is planting a misaligned canine number 3 Go out in a certain arc to create beauty for your smile friend.

Crooked teeth usually appear only in the upper jaw and form at age replacement teeth (12 to 13 years old). Although crooked teeth are usually not supportive Nothing much for chewing, but it's quite aesthetically pleasing high. Therefore, planting crooked teeth is very suitable for people with special needs Beauty needs, want a "pretty tooth" to help smile more more charming and attractive.

However, planting crooked teeth also brings quite a lot of risks latent. To shape the crooked teeth, 2 real teeth on both sides The crooked teeth will be ground down. If the performer has no hands High occupation will most likely wear down tooth enamel, the person who planted the tooth will numb afterwards. So before deciding to plant crooked teeth, You should consider it carefully.

Classification of methods of growing crooked teeth

Currently, there are quite a few methods of planting crooked teeth, each method The method has different advantages and disadvantages and the cost is very different. Most popular to be:

Composite filling

This is a simple, cheap, and complete method of creating false teeth can be removed when you no longer need the crooked teeth. Method This is also known as denture filling, because the dentist will do it It is expensive to add a layer of Composite to the existing tooth surface, creating a shape tooth-like shape.

Advantages:

  • Simple implementation, suitable for everyone
  • Does not affect the health of the dentist
  • Can be removed at any time depending on the needs of the worker
  • Cheap price

Cons:

  • A bit difficult to clean
  • Composite layer may be discolored, causing loss of aesthetics.
Porcelain veneers

This method is also known as implanting crooked teeth with porcelain dental bridges. Simply put, your fangs will be sharpened, after It is covered with 2 porcelain crowns. The first crown will cover the tooth and the second crown will be attached to the first one. The location is on the outer side on the gums.

Advantages:

  • Highly aesthetic, bright and even porcelain crown, similar in color to your real tooth enamel.
  • Fast execution time.
  • Reasonable price

Cons:

  • Because the pulp of the tooth is reduced, the real tooth will be weakened.
  • Teeth after wrapping will become more sensitive, unable to eat food too hot or too cold.
  • The way to clean porcelain teeth is quite sophisticated, if it is not cleaned properly, it will cause bad breath mouth, gingivitis, etc.
Super thin porcelain veneers

This is one of the most popular and advanced cosmetic dentistry techniques most popular today. Simply put, a porcelain patch will be Attached to the surface of your real tooth, creating the shape of a tooth khenh.

Advantages:

  • Reasonable price.

Cons: The cleaning method is quite picky, if the cleaning is wrong will cause bad breath, gingivitis, …

Trồng răng khểnh Implant

Implant is a method of permanently planting teeth, this is the technique The most modern fixed denture technique today. The texture of Implant porcelain teeth are titanium posts (replacement of real tooth roots), abutment joints and porcelain crowns (replacement of tooth surfaces). How to take care of your teeth after planting crooked teeth

If taken care of in the wrong way, graceful crooked teeth can no longer "graceful" as before. Taking care of teeth This planting is sometimes even more sophisticated and meticulous than real teeth. Specifically as follows:

  • You should practice proper oral hygiene every day. Brush your teeth at least twice a day in the morning and evening, after dinner 30 minutes minimum. In particular, when brushing to the crooked tooth, it should be light Gently and carefully to be able to clean the area around the teeth without damage to the benefit.
  • The correct way to brush your teeth is to place the toothbrush at a 45 . angle degrees, brush vertically, brush while vibrating and rotating the brush head. Should pay attention Brush force so as not to cause gums and teeth to bleed.
  • Use dental floss to clean between your teeth immediately after eating Add mouthwash or physiological saline.
  • Do not use toothpicks because they can thin teeth, recede gums, gum damage.
  • Building a healthy diet, supplementing food groups rich in calcium to help keep gums healthy, fruits rich in vitamins C will help fight tooth decay and limit inflammation of the pulp tooth.
  • Do not eat foods that are too hard or too chewy such as bones, cartilage, tendons because it can create pressure that damages the teeth.
  • Should practice the habit of eating slowly, chew carefully, gently, pay attention to chew evenly two jaws to balance muscles, ensure aesthetics later.
  • Periodic follow-up and periodic dental check-ups in the future (6 once a month) for early detection of abnormalities in gums, teeth, tissues gums and timely treatment.
